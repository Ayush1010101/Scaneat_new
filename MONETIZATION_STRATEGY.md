# Food Lens - Monetization Strategy & Business Model

## 💰 Revenue Streams

### **1. Freemium Subscription Model**

#### **Free Tier (Basic)**
- ✅ 5 food scans per day
- ✅ Basic nutritional information (macros only)
- ✅ Scan history (30 days)
- ✅ Ad-supported experience
- **Price**: $0/month

#### **Premium Tier (Pro)**
- ✅ Unlimited food scans
- ✅ Complete nutritional breakdown (vitamins, minerals, allergens)
- ✅ Full scan history (unlimited, 2+ years)
- ✅ AI meal planner
- ✅ Weekly nutrition reports
- ✅ Dietary preference tracking
- ✅ Ad-free experience
- ✅ Priority support
- **Price**: $9.99/month or $79.99/year (33% discount)

#### **Premium Plus Tier (Pro+)**
- ✅ Everything in Premium
- ✅ Personalized nutrition coaching (AI-powered)
- ✅ Meal plan generation (custom recipes)
- ✅ Integration with fitness trackers (Apple Health, Google Fit)
- ✅ Export data as PDF/CSV
- ✅ Family account (up to 5 members)
- ✅ Priority customer support (24/7)
- ✅ Early access to new features
- **Price**: $19.99/month or $179.99/year (25% discount)

#### **Enterprise Tier (B2B)**
- ✅ White-label solution
- ✅ Custom branding
- ✅ API access for restaurants/nutritionists
- ✅ Bulk user management
- ✅ Advanced analytics dashboard
- ✅ Dedicated account manager
- ✅ Custom integrations
- **Price**: Custom pricing (starting at $999/month)

## 🔑 Key Metrics to Track

### **User Metrics**
- **Total Users**: 10K → 50K → 200K
- **Active Users (DAU/MAU)**: Track daily and monthly engagement
- **Conversion Rate**: Free → Premium (target: 15-20%)
- **Churn Rate**: Target < 5% monthly
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Average revenue per user over lifetime

### **Revenue Metrics**
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Total subscription revenue
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: MRR × 12
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: Total revenue / total users
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Marketing spend / new customers
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Should be > 3:1 for healthy business

### **Product Metrics**
- **Scan Frequency**: Scans per user per day/week/month
- **Feature Usage**: % of users using each feature
- **Confidence Score**: Average AI accuracy
- **Error Rate**: % of failed scans
- **Performance**: API response time, page load time

## 💡 Pricing Psychology

### **Why These Prices?**
- **$9.99/month**: Just under $10 psychological threshold, affordable for most users
- **$79.99/year**: 33% discount incentivizes annual commitment, improves retention
- **$19.99/month**: Premium+ positioned as "luxury" tier, attracts power users
- **Free tier**: Freemium model ensures high user base for network effects

### **Conversion Optimization**
- Show feature limitations on free tier
- Highlight "Premium" badge on advanced features
- Use in-app prompts ("Upgrade to see vitamin details")
- Offer free trial (7 days) for Premium
- Limited-time discounts (20% off first month)
